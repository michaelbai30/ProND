Skills for the Irish, By the Irish

ProND is a student-led marketplace for exchanging knowledge, help, and hobbies exclusively within the Notre Dame family.

Our mission is to connect fellow students with each other through a need or a shared common interest.

With ProND, customers can securely book appointments or services from various verified skillsharers who are willing to offer a service.

Want to learn something new like the piano or need some art pieces for your new dorm room?
ProND is the answer you've been looking for!
